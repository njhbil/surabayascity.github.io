SURABAYA TOUR DESTINATION

ALUN-ALUN OF SURABAYA

The Alun-Alun of Surabaya is an iconic public space located at the heart of the largest city in East Java. Strategically situated in the city center, it is surrounded by historic and modern buildings, serving as a hub for social, cultural, and recreational activities for the people of Surabaya. This square offers a peaceful atmosphere despite being in the midst of the city’s hustle and bustle.
The well-maintained green park is a favorite spot for families to relax, exercise, or simply enjoy a serene afternoon. With its shady trees and numerous seating areas, the alun-alun provides an ideal place to unwind after a long day. Beyond relaxation, the Alun-Alun of Surabaya is often the center of various events and festivals. From Independence Day celebrations to cultural exhibitions and night markets, the square comes alive with vibrant activities. At night, the alun-alun glows with decorative lights that illuminate every corner, enhancing the beauty of Surabaya's evening atmosphere. Nearby, there are several landmarks, such as the Surabaya City Hall and the Grahadi Building, which add historical significance to the area.
Street vendors around the alun-alun offer a variety of local delicacies, such as rujak cingur, lontong balap, and es dawet, providing a taste of Surabaya’s rich culinary heritage. The Alun-Alun of Surabaya is not just a green public space, but also a symbol of community and the dynamic spirit of the city. It reflects Surabaya's progress and growth while preserving the city's traditions and fostering togetherness among its residents.

THE SURABAYA CITY HALL

The Surabaya City Hall (Balai Kota Surabaya) is a historic landmark and an important center of governance in the city. Situated in the heart of Surabaya, this grand building has stood for decades as a symbol of the city’s administrative and political power. With its distinctive colonial-era architecture, the city hall blends Dutch influences with local design, making it a striking example of the city’s rich history.
Built in the early 20th century, the Surabaya City Hall has witnessed significant moments in the city’s evolution, from the colonial period to the present. The building's exterior is adorned with large windows, towering pillars, and expansive terraces, exuding an air of authority and tradition. Its lush, well-manicured gardens provide a refreshing green space, where visitors can stroll and enjoy the surrounding beauty.
Inside, the city hall houses the offices of the city’s mayor and local government officials. It also serves as a venue for important civic functions, public meetings, and celebrations. The interior, while modernized to support the city’s operations, still retains traces of its historical charm, with elegant wooden fixtures and spacious halls.
The Surabaya City Hall is more than just an administrative building—it stands as a cultural and historical monument, representing the strength and resilience of the city. Visitors often come to admire the architecture and learn more about Surabaya's governance, while locals take pride in this iconic structure that remains central to the city's identity and development.
Surrounded by landmarks such as the Heroes Monument and the nearby Alun-Alun, the Surabaya City Hall is a key part of the urban landscape, reflecting both the city’s rich past and its vibrant future.

ROMOKALISARI ADVENTURE

Romokalisari Adventure Land is an exciting outdoor recreational park located on the outskirts of Surabaya. Nestled in a natural setting, this adventure park offers a variety of thrilling activities for families, groups of friends, and adventure enthusiasts. Its lush greenery, open spaces, and scenic landscapes make it the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.
The park offers a wide range of outdoor activities designed to satisfy different interests and adrenaline levels. Visitors can enjoy off-road ATV rides, exploring the rugged terrain, or take a more relaxed approach by kayaking on the tranquil rivers and lakes within the park. For cycling enthusiasts, there are dedicated bike trails that meander through the forested areas, providing a scenic and peaceful ride.
One of the main attractions at Romokalisari Adventure Land is its horseback riding experience, where visitors can ride horses along scenic routes, offering both a fun and therapeutic outdoor experience. For those who prefer a more leisurely day out, the park also provides picnic spots and fishing areas, where families can relax and enjoy quality time together in the fresh air.
In addition to these adventure-based activities, the park also emphasizes eco-tourism. Visitors are encouraged to appreciate and protect the natural environment, with several educational programs on sustainability and wildlife preservation available for schools and groups.
Romokalisari Adventure Land is a perfect destination for those looking to enjoy the great outdoors and indulge in adventurous activities while surrounded by the beauty of nature. It provides a refreshing and fun-filled retreat, making it a popular choice for both locals and tourists seeking adventure, relaxation, and an escape from the urban pace of Surabaya.

ATLANTIS LAND

Atlantis Land Surabaya is a vibrant and enchanting theme park located within Kenjeran Park, offering a perfect blend of water attractions, amusement rides, and colorful fantasy landscapes. As one of the most popular family destinations in Surabaya, Atlantis Land draws inspiration from the mythological world of Atlantis, transporting visitors to a magical realm filled with adventure and fun.
One of the key highlights of Atlantis Land is its expansive water park, which boasts a wide array of water slides, wave pools, and splash areas suitable for all ages. From thrilling high-speed slides to relaxing lazy rivers, the water attractions cater to both adrenaline seekers and those looking to unwind under the tropical sun. For younger visitors, there are designated kid-friendly water zones where they can safely play and enjoy mini-slides and water jets.
In addition to its water-based entertainment, Atlantis Land also features an array of exciting amusement rides. From roller coasters to carousels, the park offers attractions that are both exhilarating and family-friendly. Visitors can also explore fantasy-themed zones, where whimsical statues, colorful buildings, and themed walkways create an immersive experience that sparks the imagination.
The park is also known for its vibrant nighttime displays, where dazzling lights illuminate the park’s architecture and water features, creating a magical ambiance. Shows and live performances often take place in the evenings, adding to the park’s lively atmosphere.
Whether you’re looking for a thrilling adventure or a relaxing day out with family, Atlantis Land Surabaya offers a unique mix of entertainment, excitement, and wonder. Its diverse attractions, coupled with its captivating design, make it a must-visit destination for tourists and locals alike seeking a fun escape in the heart of Surabaya.

SURABAYA'S KOTA TUA

Surabaya’s Kota Tua, or Old Town, is a historic district that offers a glimpse into the city’s rich colonial past. Located in the northern part of Surabaya, this area is known for its well-preserved Dutch colonial buildings, narrow streets, and a unique blend of architectural styles that reflect the city’s history as a major trading port during the colonial era.
Walking through Kota Tua, visitors are transported back in time as they explore grand colonial structures that once served as trading offices, government buildings, and warehouses. These buildings, with their distinctive European façades, arched windows, and red-tiled roofs, offer a striking contrast to the modern developments of the surrounding city. Some of the most notable buildings include the historic House of Sampoerna, which today serves as a museum, and the old Surabaya Harbor, a key player in Indonesia’s maritime trade during the Dutch East Indies period.
Kota Tua is not just about architecture, but also about culture and heritage. The area is home to vibrant street markets, where visitors can experience local cuisine, arts, and crafts. Strolling through these markets, one can enjoy traditional dishes such as nasi rawon or rujak cingur, while appreciating the daily life of locals who still carry on the traditions passed down through generations.
In recent years, Kota Tua has become a focal point for heritage preservation efforts, as the city works to restore and revitalize many of the historic buildings while maintaining their original charm. As a result, the area has seen an influx of cafes, art spaces, and galleries that add a modern touch to this historic district, making it a lively and artistic hub.
Kota Tua Surabaya offers a unique and fascinating journey through the city’s history, combining the charm of colonial-era architecture with the lively culture of modern-day Surabaya. It stands as a reminder of the city’s role as a key player in Indonesia’s historical and cultural landscape, and remains a favorite destination for tourists and locals alike who seek to explore the soul of old Surabaya.
